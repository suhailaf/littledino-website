Privacy Policy

How Little Dino handles data and privacy

This privacy policy explains the type of information Little Dino may collect, how it may be used, and how families can better understand the app’s approach to privacy.

1. Introduction

Little Dino is designed to help children discover age-appropriate books and videos in a safer and more guided way. Protecting user trust, especially for children and families, is important to the Little Dino platform.

2. Information we may collect

Depending on platform features, Little Dino may collect limited information such as:

  • Basic account details such as name, email address, or parent account information
  • Child profile details such as age range, reading level, or interests
  • App interaction data such as search history, favourites, and recommendation activity
  • Technical information required for app functionality and security

3. How information may be used

Collected data may be used to:

  • Provide personalised recommendations for books and videos
  • Improve chatbot responses and app usability
  • Support parental controls and child-safe filtering
  • Monitor app performance, reliability, and security

4. Child safety and parent awareness

Little Dino is intended to support a more guided digital experience for children. Parent-related controls, age filtering, and safer discovery features are designed to help reduce exposure to unsuitable content.

5. Data storage and security

Little Dino aims to use secure systems and access controls to protect user data. Security measures may include authenticated access, protected databases, validation rules, and restricted administrative controls.

6. Sharing of information

Little Dino does not aim to sell personal data. Information would only be shared when needed for platform functionality, legal compliance, or essential service operation.

7. User choices

Users or parents may be able to:

  • Review account information
  • Update child preferences
  • Manage favourites or history
  • Request account deletion where applicable

8. Changes to this policy

This privacy policy may be updated as the Little Dino platform grows. Any future updates should reflect the latest data handling and platform features.

9. Contact

For privacy-related questions, users may contact:
admin@littledino.com
