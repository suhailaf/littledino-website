Features

What makes Little Dino special?

The platform is designed to help children find content in a simple, safe, and engaging way.

💬

Chatbot Recommendations

Children can describe what they want in simple words and receive helpful book and video suggestions.

📚

Curated Book Library

Books are presented with title, summary, image, and age suitability for easier selection.

🎥

Video Discovery

Kids can also receive educational video recommendations based on their interests.

⭐

Personalised Picks

Recommendations can be influenced by age, reading level, interests, and favourites.

🛡️

Safety First

The platform focuses on child-appropriate content and guided exploration for a more reassuring user experience.

📱

Mobile Friendly

Little Dino is designed as a simple mobile-first experience that is easy for children to navigate.

How It Works

A simple journey for young users

The experience is designed to reduce confusion and make content discovery fun.

1

Ask the chatbot

A child enters a simple prompt, such as a topic, genre, or type of story they want.

2

Get recommendations

The app suggests age-appropriate books and videos based on the request and profile details.

3

Explore safely

Users can read, save favourites, and continue discovering content in a guided environment.

Safety

About the project

Little Dino is a student project that explores how a chatbot-based mobile application can help children discover books and educational videos more naturally than traditional browsing alone.

This website acts as a preliminary marketing prototype and can later be expanded with app screenshots, team details, download links, and additional branding content.
