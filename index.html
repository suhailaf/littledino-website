Safety

Built with child-friendly discovery in mind

Little Dino is planned as a safe and supportive environment that promotes educational exploration while keeping the interface simple and approachable for both children and parents.

  • Curated content sources: Content is selected to support a safer and more suitable discovery experience.
  • Age-appropriate recommendations: Suggestions can be aligned to the child’s age group and reading level.
  • Guided exploration: The chatbot reduces the need for random browsing by leading children toward relevant options.
  • Educational focus: The platform prioritises meaningful and useful learning content.
  • Parent-control-ready direction: The design supports future parent settings such as limits, preferences, and filtering.
  • Structured content management: Admin-managed content helps maintain consistency, quality, and child suitability.

Why parents may like it

The platform aims to balance independence for children with safer discovery through curated content, clearer structure, and a guided search experience.
